About CRPR

The Computing Research Projects Repository (CRPR) is a centralized platform for showcasing and cataloging cutting-edge research in computing.

Our Story

How It All Began

CRPR was founded in 2024 with the goal of creating a centralized hub for computing research. Our passionate team built a platform for collaboration and innovation.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a future where CRPR becomes the go-to platform for researchers, students, and industry professionals to access, share, and collaborate on cutting-edge computing projects.

Our Mission & Values

Advancing research in computing with a focus on innovation, collaboration, and transparency.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of computing research with new ideas.

Collaboration

We believe in teamwork and interdisciplinary research for breakthroughs.

Transparency

We are committed to open, ethical, and accessible research.

How to Publish a Research Project

Eligibility

To publish a research project, you must be:

  • A student at JKUAT OR
  • A holder of a valid JKUAT email address
Step 1: Log In / Sign Up

Use your JKUAT email to log in or create an account.

Step 2: Update Your Profile

Add your name, and a profile image.

Step 3: Create a Project

Click the "Create a Project" button and fill in the required details.

Step 4: Project Listing

Your project will be listed under "Ongoing Projects".

Step 5: Manage Your Project

Update, cancel, or mark your project as complete.

Step 6: Completion

Once marked complete, no further edits can be made.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Computing Research Projects Repository (CRPR)? +
The Computing Research Projects Repository (CRPR) is a centralized platform designed to catalog and showcase ongoing research projects conducted within the Department of Computing.
Who can access the CRPR? +
CRPR provides researchers, students, and stakeholders with easy access to project information, resources, outcomes, and collaborations.
How can prospective students benefit from CRPR? +
Prospective students can explore the diverse range of research projects within the department, understand current research trends, and identify potential mentors or research groups they might want to join.
What type of research projects are included in CRPR? +
CRPR includes research projects from various computing domains such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software engineering, data science, and more. Each project is detailed with information on its objectives, outcomes, and ongoing collaborations.
How can industry partners engage with CRPR? +
Industry partners can engage with CRPR by exploring the repository to identify potential collaboration opportunities, funding research projects, and connecting with researchers and students working on innovative solutions within their fields of interest.
Can I search for specific projects in CRPR? +
Yes, CRPR includes a search feature that allows users to search for specific projects by keywords, domains, or researchers. This makes it easy to find relevant projects and information quickly.
